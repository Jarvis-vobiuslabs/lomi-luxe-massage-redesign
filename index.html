About Leta
Certified Holistic Lomi Lomi Massage Practitioner

Hi, I'm Leta

Lomi Lomi, also known as Loving Hands massage, is a deeply restorative practice rooted in Hawaiian healing traditions. It combines long, rhythmic flowing strokes — inspired by the gentle motion of ocean waves — with warm nut oils, intentional breathing techniques, and graceful movement.

The goal is harmony: to soothe the body, quiet the mind, support circulation, and invite deep, genuine relaxation. Each session is tailored to your needs, so you always leave feeling exactly as you should.
